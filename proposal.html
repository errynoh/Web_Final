WE MET IN A BAR...

Abel and Elsie met in 2009, during their first year of school at the University of Virginia. They were in three courses together, but bonded over their mutual affection for the music of Ellie Goulding. They grew closer over their first year, and finally, after some coaching from their mutual friends, became a couple the next fall. After six years, and two cross-country moves together, they are getting married!
